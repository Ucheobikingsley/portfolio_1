Background

Freight first is that intermediary freight broker between a shipper who has goods to transport and a receiver waiting to get a hand on that good. There are plenty of brokerages out there already, but freight first specializes in finding optimal routes for sensitive cargo that requires ongoing monitoring and comprehensive insurance coverage. In the process of pitching a new road map for investors, the management reached out for a brand identity to solidify the presentation. Past the initial discussion on the business side and scope of the project; the team and I came up with something that was effective and timeless for the company.

Problem

Being a company that is built on the foundations of trust and security with its clients, having an identity that resonates the brand’s core values and goals, is imperative. This was why the team needed something that gives customers a sense of trust that their delivery is handled by professional agents that understand how to transport cargo using safe and secure practices. Also requested by the team is a logo that’s not necessarily eye catching and colorful but has to convey the company’s industry experience and professional services. The logo should also be easy to transition across different media.

Process

Like any project, the process started off with sketching. Rough concepts on paper and digital format. The goal was to create a logo that conveyed the team's idea of a “BOLD, RELIABLE, and TIMELESS” brand. After lots of exploration, using three elements of the brand, a few selected variants were presented to the team. The elements are the letter F, number 1, and a Circle to represent the binoculars lens used for monitoring transportation from Point A to Point B.

Typography

Major Font used for this project is called Zona Pro. This font is a geometric sans - serif which draws heavy inspiration from geometric style faces having clean and highly readable shapes, hence the fit with this brand. Also Helvetica Condensed is used as the adjunct font to give visual balance.
