Muslimah Yusuf

BRAND:Philips Electronics

PROJECT: The Spirit of giving Campaign

CHANNELS: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube

ROLE:Digital Strategy

Background

The end of the year is usually colored with festivity, food and drinks, entertainment, family and corporate events. A key thing that features during this period is gifting among colleagues, business partners, families and friends.

problem

How do we insert the more pricey Philips range of products into this cultural festivity?

strategy

Our TA is saddled with lots of responsibilities and decisions to make in their everyday life. Choosing gifts can be a stressful experience especially when our target audience have a diverse group of people they want to get gifts to and they want to make sure they are gifting right.

Insight: I feel a sense of pride when people appreciate my gifts. I know that it will make me be remembered warmly.

Strategy:Communicate Philips as the brand that makes gifting easier. With the range of kitchen appliances, personal care and garment care, Philips takes the pain out of shopping by providing an array of gifts that outlasts the season

Creative ideas

Beyond the Season

The Result

This campaign made the far reaching Philips range of products seem relatable to a lot of ordinary Nigerians. Marrying the brand with cultural conversations made people better understand the premium pricing which increased the value of the brand. 45% more sales than anticipated was recorded as more people saved up to buy the brand.
