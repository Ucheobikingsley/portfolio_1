Strength to dream bournvita campaign

BRAND:Cadbury Bournvita

PROJECT:Strength to Dream

CHANNELS:TV, Radio, OOH, Digital (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, YouTube)

ROLE:Strategy

Background

For years, Cadbury Bournvita had been losing steam with the Nigerian audience. What used to be the category leader became the struggling second brand in the category.

problem

What is missing in the connection with the audience? How do we re-position the brand in a new light to appeal to the modern target audience?

strategy

The modern Nigerian Mum is drastically different from the Mothers of the past. Nowadays, Mums are more open minded and willing to allow their kids explore and try new things; something they didn’t have when they were children. Because they value this freedom, they realize they have to guide and prepare their children on the way to success.

Insight: Moms have taken it upon themselves to help nurture their children’s dream even through the unconventional.

Strategy: Position Bournvita as the beverage that that gives children the support to achieve their dreams through the provision of necessary immunity supporting nutrients in the beverage. Thereby partnering with mums to provide an all-round preparation for their kids to win in life and not just in class.

Creative ideas

Strength to Dream.

The campaign appealed to the main concern of modern Nigerian mums which is to find ways they can prepare their children for the increasingly competitive world.

The platform of “Strength to Dream” has been cascaded into other touch points such as the “Cadbury Bournvita BootCamp” where young kids are awarded scholarships to improve their literacy to lead with digital skills. This is consistent with the brand’s efforts in collaborating with parents to help their children pursue their dreams.

The result

This campaign brought life back to the struggling brand, it repositioned the brand with a fresh look and tone that resonated with the target audience. Seeing an increase in digital conversations and engagements, the brand recorded increase in sales after the campaign launch.
