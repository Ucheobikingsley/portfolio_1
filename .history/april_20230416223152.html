April fool 2021

BRAND:Ogilvy Africa, Nigeria

PROJECT:April Fool

ROLE:Ideation

BACKGROUND EXECUTION

We sent a brief to the Ogilvy team 24 hours before April 1st, 2021. It stated that the agency had been contracted to be a part of the West African Launch of Nike 3 lucky staff would join the team in California to start planning They would be considered part of the lucky staff if they come up with good launch ideas.

Insight

Talented Nigerians are ready to take advantage of opportunities to showcase their skills on a global stage.

Execution

Utilizing "April Fool's Day" we pranked Ogilvy Staff to create a campaign for Nike global for a chance to win an all expense paid trip to California.

Result

After a good laugh and snacks to soothe feelings, this served as one of the bonding moments that amplified the "agency fun culture" leading more creative works to be delivered to our clients.
