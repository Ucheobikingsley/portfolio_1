Muslimah Yusuf

BRAND:Cadbury_Hot_Chocolate

PROJECT:Enjoy the Cadbury Goodness

ROLE:Strategy

Background

For a few years, Cadbury_Hot_Chocolate had been in the Nigerian market riding on the global Cadbury master brand without any major product communication. However, with the rise of category competitors, the brand needed to position itself as the category leader

Problem

How does the brand differentiate itself from the influx of traditional and new brands while connecting with its’ consumers?

strategy

Nigerians are tea lovers. One would say as Americans drink coffee, so do Nigerians drink tea. However, Cadbury_Hot_Chocolate is more than just tea, it’s by far the frothiest chocolate drink in the market that brings a sense of calm and balance when it is consumed.

Insight: Nigerians put so much effort in achieving their goals, hence they often crave for moments to unwind and indulge.

Strategy: Position our Cadbury_Hot_Chocolate as the perfect reward and Indulgent companion for every well-deserved unwinding moment.

Creative idea

Enjoy the Cadbury Goodness.

The campaign called on consumers to enjoy the unique Cadbury_Hot_Chocolate while calling out it’s functional attributes that makes it stand out from other brands in the market and the perfect drink for moments of indulgence and rest.

We further played on the theme of “indulgence” to create thematic campaigns to continue to inspire “a little more Cadbury Goodness in the world”. From project purple which was a reward system during the Christmas holidays to assist economic challenged people, to the various digital conversations calling especially young Nigerians to remember to take a break.

Additionally, media platforms were used to amplify the expression “Cadbury Goodness” around moments of indulgence leveraging our consumers interest in Movies and Lifestyle

Result

Increase in overall TOMA with the target audience that translated to increase in digital conversations, engagements and sales. Consumers now identify with what the brand stands for as it relates to modern issues of mental health and rest.
