Coca-Cola Ginjaaah you flow by mayorkun

Capacity - Brand Strategy

Background

Coca cola was to be globally positioned as the first beverage to come to mind as a daily energizer in a bid to build a long standing habit of drinking Coca-Cola during moments of physical and mental slump during mid-day.

it was a global campaign that had to be adapted for the Nigerian audience to fit into their lifestyle so as to better resonate with them

Target Audience

They are young adults that are committed to building the life they desire. Hence, they are dissatisfied with the country's conventional systems, and are disconnected from it. However, they are connecting to more possibilities. armed with digital + mobile, they have focused on themselves for success, significance and recognition.

They are particularly interested in any form of African content, feeling proud especially the impact of the Nigeran Music industry on the global stage.

PainPoint

The on-the-go generation is not always on the go; afternoon slump sets in, routine gets boring, hustle gets harsh, energy is low, morale is down.

Insight

Nigerians don't wallow in down moments. They turn to their friends, pump up the music, switch on the internet and grab their favorite drink.
