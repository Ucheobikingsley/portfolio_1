Muslimah Yusuf

BRAND:Coca Cola

PROJECT: UpLift Global Campaign

ROLE:Strategy

Background

The global uplift campaign was to position Coca Cola as the drink of choice during moments of physical and mental slump during mid-day.

problem

How do we position this global campaign to make it relevant to the Nigerian audience?

Strategy

With the Nigerian audience faced with environmental and economic challenges; they struggle with limitations that makes the journey of life twice as hard. But they are determined to create the life they desire. Empowered by digital and tech, using social media as their stage they believe there’s power in the collective and music serves as a social lubricant to their events and movements.


insight: Routines gets boring, hustle gets harsh, energy is low, morale is down but Nigerians never wallow in down moments for long. They turn to their friends, both offline and online and pump up the music.


Strategy: Coca-Cola wants to reduce the friction and stress of building a great life in Nigeria. The brand wants you to Spark your Rhythm, never losing yourself to the grind of everyday life.

Creative idea

Ginjaaah Your Flow.

Which loosely translates to “More energy” portrays the infectious energy and buoyant spirit of young Nigerians and inspires them to remain Ginjaaah’d every day. As Nigerians say it, Coke ginjaaah's your senses. It wakes you up, igniting your senses with refreshment and taste that sparks your daily flow.


Through mapping out different artist’s ability to ignite our target audience, Mayorkun was chosen to communicate this message in the most exciting way. The artiste is known for commanding attention with his catchy melodies and at the end of the day, Coca-Cola is the energy, Music is the frequency. Together it creates positive vibes and energy.

Coca-cola is the energy, Music is the frequency.

Together it creates positive vibes and energy.

The Result

Logging over 395,000 views on YouTube in its first week, the song became a trend on social media platforms with people showing their dancing steps and how they keep themselves motivated in their daily lives.

Festivals where Mayorkun performed during the campaign time-frame recorded an increase in the sales of Coca-Cola.
